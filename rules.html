A Few guidelines to Appraiser

  1. Only Form I has to be sent to the Director. Form II is to be retained with the concerned Department.
  2. In part B following rule is to be applied in finalising the marks.
    Score inPart B = Maximum of (B1,B2,B3) x 0.20 + Sum of rest of (B1,B2,B3) x 0.15
    This calculation is done for every faculty in the department and highest score is scaled down to 50. Rest of the scores are correspondingly scaled down. A sample calculation is given below.

Faculty B1 B2 B3 Score in Part B Final Score out of 50
A 100 75 40 37.25 46.85
B 50 60 80 32.50 40.88
C 60 80 85 38.00 47.80
D 150 20 45 39.75 50.00
  1. In Part B1, faculty are advised to give correct data. Any misrepresentation of data would be viewed seriously.
  2. For faculty on approved leave exceeding 2 month, retain the assessment of previous year, but not exceeding A+.
  3. New faculty joining in the middle of the semester are to be considered under A+.
  4. Assistant Professors/ Assistant Professors (on Contract), shall be awarded with the best of points, obtained from (i) 75% weightage for Part A and 25 % of weightage for Part B OR (ii) 50 % weightage each for Part A and Part B, in their first three years of service with MIT.
  5. Faculty in the cadre Assistant Professors/ Assistant Professors- Senior scale with > 5 years of service with MIT are expected to register for PhD, failing which their PMS Grading will be limited at 'A+'. Faculty who fail to register for PhD in spite of 3 such chances, will be limited at 'A' in their PMS Grading.
  6. Research progress of faculty registered for PhD with MU is to be assessed through at least one DAC meeting/ year failing which their PMS grading will be limited at A+.
  7. Research progress of faculty registered for PhD outside MU will be assessed based on progress report submitted through their guides failing which their PMS grading will be limited at A+.
  8. All eligible faculty with PhD shall apply for guide-ship OR co-guide-ship with MU.
  9. Awarding full points for PhD Research progress is subject to satisfactory fulfillment of progress reports and publications as per requirement.
  10. Awarding full points to a guide/co-guide for PhD guidance is subject to satisfactory performance of research scholars working under him or her.
  11. In awarding points in Part A, items in student feedback on faculty may be referred. A sample mapping is given below.
